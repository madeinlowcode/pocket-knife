# Product Guide

## Competitor Teardown

**Framework:**
| Category | Questions to Answer |
|----------|---------------------|
| Positioning | Who is their ICP? What's their unique value prop? |
| Product | Top 3 features? What's missing? What's overcomplicated? |
| Pricing | Model (seat/usage/flat)? Price points? Free tier? |
| Growth | Primary acquisition channel? Content strategy? |
| Weakness | 1-star reviews say what? Forum complaints? |

**Data sources:**
- G2/Capterra: Real user reviews and ratings
- Job postings: What they're building next
- Changelog/blog: Recent priorities
- Reddit/HN: Unfiltered user sentiment
- Wayback Machine: How positioning evolved

## Customer Persona

**Persona template:**
```
## [Persona Name] — [Job Title]

**Demographics:** [Age range, company size, industry]

**Goals:**
- Primary: [What they're trying to achieve]
- Secondary: [Supporting goals]

**Pain Points:**
- [Pain 1: specific, not vague]
- [Pain 2]
- [Pain 3]

**Buying triggers:** [What makes them look for a solution?]
**Objections:** [What prevents purchase?]
**Success metric:** [How do they measure if the product is working?]

**Quote:** "[Verbatim from user research or synthesized from reviews]"
```

**Research methods:** User interviews (5-8 per segment), support ticket analysis, churn survey analysis, sales call recordings

## Product Changelog

**Format:**
```
## [Version or Date]

### New
- [Feature]: [what it does and why it matters to the user]

### Improved
- [Feature]: [what changed and the impact]

### Fixed
- [Bug]: [symptom that was fixed, not internal ticket name]
```

**Writing rules:**
- User benefit first: "You can now..." not "We added..."
- Specific: "Exports 10x faster for files over 100MB" not "Performance improved"
- Avoid internal jargon: No ticket numbers, no code terms
- Include screenshots for UI changes

## Product Hunt Launch

**Pre-launch checklist (2 weeks before):**
- [ ] Maker account verified and active
- [ ] Product page complete: tagline, description, gallery (5+ images), video (optional)
- [ ] Hunters identified and messaged (find via PH Hunter database)
- [ ] Audience notification list ready (newsletter, Slack communities, personal network)
- [ ] Launch day content prepared (Twitter, LinkedIn posts)

**Launch day timeline (PST):**
| Time | Action |
|------|--------|
| 12:01am | Go live (midnight PST) |
| 8am | Send email to subscribers |
| 9am | Post on LinkedIn and Twitter |
| 12pm | Post in relevant Slack/Discord communities |
| 3pm | Follow up with engaged commenters |
| 6pm | End-of-day update post |

**Response template for comments:**
"Thanks [Name]! [Answer their specific question or acknowledge their point]. [1 sentence about a related feature or context they'd find interesting]."
