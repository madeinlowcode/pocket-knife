# AI Marketing Video Production Guide

## Core Principle
Every marketing video answers one question for the viewer: "Why should I care?" Structure everything around that answer.

---

## Script Architecture

### The 3-Part Framework
```
HOOK (0–3 sec)   → Stop the scroll
BODY (4–45 sec)  → Deliver the value
CTA (last 3 sec) → Tell them what to do next
```

### Hook Types (choose one per video)
- **Pain hook**: "Still spending 3 hours editing videos manually?"
- **Curiosity hook**: "Most marketers get this completely wrong."
- **Result hook**: "We grew to 50k subscribers in 90 days. Here's how."
- **Bold claim hook**: "Cold email is not dead — you're just doing it wrong."
- **Visual hook**: Unexpected visual with no text for 1.5 sec, then reveal

### Body Structure Options

**Problem → Agitate → Solve (PAS)**
1. Name the specific problem (5 sec)
2. Make it worse — describe the real cost (10 sec)
3. Introduce the solution naturally (20 sec)
4. Show proof or demo (10 sec)

**Before → After → Bridge (BAB)**
1. Describe where the audience is now (before)
2. Paint the picture of the desired outcome (after)
3. Your product/service is the bridge

**Story Arc**
1. Character faces a familiar struggle
2. Discovery or turning point
3. Transformation with your solution

## Pacing Guidelines

| Platform   | Ideal Pace         | Cut Frequency     |
|------------|--------------------|-------------------|
| TikTok     | Very fast          | Every 1–2 sec     |
| Reels      | Fast               | Every 2–3 sec     |
| YouTube Shorts | Fast          | Every 2–4 sec     |
| YouTube (long) | Moderate     | Every 5–10 sec    |
| LinkedIn   | Moderate-slow      | Every 4–8 sec     |

**Pacing rules:**
- Remove every second of dead air — no pause longer than 1.5 sec in short-form
- Use jump cuts aggressively in talking-head content
- Change visual (B-roll, text overlay, zoom) every 3 sec minimum for retention
- End sentences before cutting — avoid cutting mid-breath

## Music Selection Framework

### Mood-to-Message Matching
| Video Tone      | Music Character              | BPM Range  |
|-----------------|------------------------------|------------|
| Inspirational   | Uplifting, swelling          | 90–110     |
| Urgent/promo    | Driving, percussive          | 120–140    |
| Trust/brand     | Clean, minimal piano/acoustic| 70–90      |
| Tech/product    | Electronic, clean synth      | 100–120    |
| Playful         | Bouncy, fun, staccato        | 110–130    |

### Volume Mixing
- Music sits at -18 to -22 dB under voiceover
- Music fades to -25 dB during key spoken moments
- No music during CTA — let the voice carry it alone
- Fade out music 0.5 sec before video ends, not at the same time

### Licensing Checklist
- [ ] Royalty-free source confirmed (Artlist, Epidemic Sound, Pixabay)
- [ ] Commercial license covers platform distribution
- [ ] No ContentID flag risk for YouTube

## Platform-Specific Formats

### TikTok
- **Aspect ratio**: 9:16 (1080×1920)
- **Duration**: 15–60 sec optimal; 3-min max
- **Safe zones**: Keep text 150px from top and bottom edges
- **Caption style**: Large font, high contrast, center-positioned
- **Hook rule**: First frame must work as a still image thumbnail
- **Trend hooks**: Mirror native TikTok audio trends when relevant

### Instagram Reels
- **Aspect ratio**: 9:16 (1080×1920)
- **Duration**: 15–90 sec sweet spot
- **Safe zones**: 250px from bottom (button overlay), 150px from top
- **Cover frame**: First frame doubles as grid thumbnail — brand it
- **Caption**: Subtitles mandatory (85% watch on mute)

### YouTube (standard)
- **Aspect ratio**: 16:9 (1920×1080)
- **Duration**: 8–15 min for ads/educational; 60–90 sec for shorts-style
- **Thumbnail**: Custom — 1280×720, <2 MB, max contrast faces + text
- **Hook rule**: Deliver on your title promise within the first 30 sec
- **End screens**: Reserve last 20 sec for YouTube end screen elements

### YouTube Shorts
- **Aspect ratio**: 9:16 (1080×1920)
- **Duration**: Under 60 sec
- **Loop potential**: Write scripts that benefit from replaying

### LinkedIn Video
- **Aspect ratio**: 16:9 or 1:1 (square performs well in feed)
- **Duration**: 30–90 sec for awareness; up to 10 min for thought leadership
- **Subtitles**: Mandatory — auto-captions or baked-in
- **Tone**: Professional but not stiff — conversational authority
- **CTA style**: Direct and specific ("Comment 'GUIDE' to get the template")

## CTA Best Practices

### CTA Formula
```
[Action verb] + [specific object] + [reason/benefit]
```
Examples:
- "Download the free template below to cut your editing time in half."
- "Comment 'YES' and I'll send you the full script."
- "Follow for Part 2 — dropping Friday."

### CTA Placement Rules
- Short-form: CTA at the very end (last 3–5 sec)
- Long-form YouTube: Soft CTA at 30% mark + hard CTA at end
- Never put CTA before delivering value
- One CTA per video — do not stack actions

## Pre-Production Checklist
- [ ] Target audience defined (1 specific person, not a demographic)
- [ ] Single core message identified (one sentence)
- [ ] Hook tested — would YOU stop scrolling?
- [ ] Script read aloud and timed
- [ ] B-roll list prepared to match script beats
- [ ] Music licensed and pre-selected
- [ ] Platform format confirmed before filming

## Post-Production Checklist
- [ ] Captions/subtitles added
- [ ] Color graded consistently
- [ ] Audio levels normalized (-14 LUFS for online)
- [ ] Safe zones verified per platform
- [ ] CTA visible and readable on mobile
- [ ] First 3 sec work without audio
- [ ] Exported at correct resolution and codec (H.264, AAC)
